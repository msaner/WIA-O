Almost gone...

But not in a really cool puff of smoke ninja disappearing act kind of way. In this fast paced world of bullet trains, cell phones, the internet and air travel the passage of time is accelerated. While we enjoy the modern conveniences afforded us by advanced technology not everyone is benefiting. Long ago in an age when chivalry, honor and courage reigned an elite group of warriors thrived. They are, the Ninja. The ninja class has little to do with Hollywoods mythical depictions of masked assassins wearing all black sneaking through the night. These were and still are a class of warrior known for their stealth, and dedication.

Most of the world is complacent in believing that the ninja went the way of the horse drawn carriage or tall ships of the ancient age. But this is not so. Their numbers are dwindling and without immidiate action by people like you they will vanish from the earth and their culture, art and combat techniques will go with them. The time to act is now, this brave class of men has protected the human race for centuries and now they need you to help them.
